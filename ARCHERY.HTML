NEON ARCHERY

Master the futuristic bow. Clear hits grant extra arrows and power. Tap inside the game board to release.

How to Play

  • ⚡ Tap or Click: Press anywhere inside the glowing game board to release your neon energy arrow.
  • 🏹 Hit the Moving Beacon: Time your shots perfectly to strike the traveling vertical neon target on the right.
  • 🎯 Aim for Bullseyes: Hit the center of the bar to get a BULLSEYE (+10 points & +2 arrows). Great hits near the center grant (+5 points & +1 arrow).
  • ⏱️ Cooldown Watch: Do not wait too long! The auto-shoot cooldown bar fills constantly. It will release an arrow automatically once complete.
